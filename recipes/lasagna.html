Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings such as ragu (ground meats and tomato souce), vegetables, cheeses and seasonings and spices, like Italian seasoning, such as garlic, oregano and basil.

For this recipe, we are essentially making a thick, meaty tomato sauce and layering that with noodles and cheese into a casserole.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
  6. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
